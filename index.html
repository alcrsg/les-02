Pasta al Limone e Basilico

Gennaro Contaldo's famous lemon pasta.

Ingredients

  • 500g dried linguine
  • 3 Sicilian lemons
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 75g Parmesan cheese
  • 75g Pecorino cheese
  • 1 bunch of fresh basil
  • 1 handful of rocket

Method

  1. Cook the linguine in a pan of boiling salted water according to the packet intructions, then drain and return to the pan.
